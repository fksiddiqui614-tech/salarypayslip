How to Use

This is a online salary payslip calculator and generator tool for personal,educational,and demo purposes only.It is not a payroll service. online salary payslip calculator helps you for a simple calculation in just a few simple steps. Start by uploading your company logo, which will appear on both the payslip and the downloaded PDF. Next, enter the employee’s details such as name, designation, employee ID, and pay date. After that, input the basic salary along with allowances including House Rent Allowance (HRA), Medical Allowance, and any other applicable allowances. You can also add bonuses and deductions if applicable. Enter the tax percentage, and the tool will automatically calculate the tax amount for you. Select your preferred currency from the dropdown menu. Once all details are filled, click on the “Calculate Salary” button to instantly view the payslip. After reviewing the details, click on “Download PDF” to get a ready-to-use, system-generated payslip.

FAQs

Q1: Is this salary payslip calculator & generator for everyone?
Yes, this tool is for everyone worldwide to use. You can generate and download unlimited payslips.

Yes, the downloaded PDF will exactly match the payslip, including employee details, salary breakdown, allowances, and company logo.

The tax is calculated automatically based on the tax percentage you enter. The system applies the percentage to the gross salary and deducts it accordingly.

Yes, you can easily select your preferred currency from the currency dropdown before calculating the salary.
