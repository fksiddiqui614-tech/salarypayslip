How to Use

This Salary Pay Slip Generator helps you create a professional and accurate payslip in just a few simple steps.  Start by uploading your company logo, which will appear on both the payslip preview and the downloaded PDF.  Next, enter the employee’s details such as name, designation, employee ID, and pay date. After that, input the basic salary along with allowances including House Rent Allowance (HRA), Medical Allowance,  and any other applicable allowances. You can also add bonuses and deductions if applicable. Enter the tax percentage, and the tool will automatically calculate the tax amount for you. Select your preferred currency from the dropdown menu. Once all details are filled, click on the “Calculate Salary” button to instantly view the payslip preview. After reviewing the details, click on “Download PDF” to get a ready-to-use, system-generated payslip.
