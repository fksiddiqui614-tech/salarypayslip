FAQs

Q1: Is this salary pay slip calculator free to use?

A1: Yes, it is completely free. You can calculate salaries and download pay slips without any charges.

Q2: Can I upload my company logo?

A2: Yes, you can upload your company logo in the "Add Company Logo Here" section. Once uploaded, it will appear on the preview and PDF.

Q3: Can I change the currency for the salary?

A3: Yes, select your preferred currency from the dropdown before calculating the salary.

Q4: Will the PDF format be the same as the preview?

A4: Yes, the PDF will match the preview exactly, including allowances breakdown, employee details, and logo.

Q5: Is the tool mobile-friendly?

A5: Yes, the tool is fully responsive and works on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

How to Use

  1. Upload your company logo.
  2. Enter employee details: Name, Designation, ID, and Pay Date.
  3. Enter Basic Salary and Allowances.
  4. Enter Bonus, Deductions, and Tax.
  5. Select Currency.
  6. Click "Calculate Salary" to see preview.
  7. Verify details, then click "Download PDF".
