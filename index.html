How to Use

This online salary payslip calculator and generator is designed for worldwide users. Start by uploading your company logo, which will appear on both the payslip and the downloaded PDF. Enter employee details such as name, designation, employee ID, and pay date. Add the basic salary along with allowances like HRA, medical, and other benefits. Include bonuses, deductions, and tax percentage if applicable. Click on Calculate Salary to instantly preview the payslip. Once satisfied, click Download PDF to save a system-generated payslip.
