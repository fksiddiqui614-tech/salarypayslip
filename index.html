How to Use Salary Pay Slip Generator

Step 1: Fill Employee Details

Enter Employee Name, Designation, Employee ID, and select the Date.

Step 2: Fill Salary Components

Enter Basic Salary, Allowances, Overtime Pay, Bonuses, Late Days and Penalty per Day, Other Deductions, and Tax Percentage.

Step 3: Select Currency

Click "Select Currency" and choose your currency from the scrollable list.

Step 4: Calculate Salary

Click Calculate Salary to view net salary with breakdown in a professional pay slip.

Step 5: Print or Download

Click Print Pay Slip for hard copy or Download PDF to save professional PDF payslip.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: Can I calculate salary for multiple dates?

Yes, select the required date, fill salary details, and calculate. Each calculation generates a new payslip.

Q2: Is the PDF professional format?

Yes, PDF includes company name, employee details, salary breakdown, net salary, and signature rows in corporate style.

Q3: Can I include bonuses and overtime?

Yes, any bonuses, incentives, or overtime pay are included in net salary calculation.

Q4: Is this tool free?

Yes, completely free worldwide.

Q5: Can I select my local currency?

Yes, select from the currency dropdown with 200+ worldwide currencies. Scroll to find your currency.
