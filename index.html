How to Use

This is a online salary payslip calculator and generator tool for personal, educational, and demo purposes only. It is not a payroll service. Start by uploading your company logo, which will appear on both the payslip and the downloaded PDF. Enter the employee’s details such as name, designation, employee ID, and pay date. Input the basic salary along with allowances including HRA, Medical, and any other applicable allowances. Add bonuses and deductions if applicable. Enter the tax percentage, and the tool will calculate the tax amount automatically. Select your preferred currency from the dropdown. Click on “Calculate Salary” to view the payslip, then click “Download PDF” to get a ready-to-use, system-generated payslip.

FAQs

Q1: Is this salary payslip calculator & generator for everyone?
Yes, this tool is for everyone worldwide. You can generate and download unlimited payslips.

Q2: Will the downloaded PDF match the payslip?
Yes, the downloaded PDF will exactly match the payslip, including employee details, salary breakdown, allowances, and company logo.

Q3: How is the tax calculated in this tool?
The tax is calculated automatically based on the tax percentage entered. The system applies it to the gross salary.

Q4: Can I change the currency for different employees?
Yes, you can select your preferred currency from the dropdown before calculating the salary.

Q5: Is this salary payslip generator mobile-friendly?
Yes, the tool is fully responsive and works on mobile phones, tablets, and desktops.
